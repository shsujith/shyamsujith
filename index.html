About Me

The core value which I place importance upon is the law of equivalent exchange. The more you work, the more abundance you will spawn in life.

Being an inquisitive person, I was drawn to technology. Techonology is like a giant piece of puzzle. The beauty in how everything fits together and works seamlessly boggles my mind.Understanding the nuances of technology gives me immense pleasure. So I was drawn to computer science from a very young age. And thus my journey began. A professor in my college inspired me to get into programming and I was hooked instantenously.

But it was only till I learnt about Cyber Security, I found my true calling. Aeons were spent researching about different vulnerabilites, the ways to exploit them and to mitigate them. Time used to flow without an end when I'm immersed in my work. Experimenting with different tools, methods in exploiting is my favorite thing to do. Each bug is unique and each time it requires a different, delicate approach especially when the environment is a production one. It's almost poetic in a unconventional way.
